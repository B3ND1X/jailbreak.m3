checkra1n is an ultimate jailbreak tool with support for most generations of iOS devices starting from iPhone 4S (A5 chip) to iPhone 8 and iPhone X (A11 chip). Download Checkra1n (Mac OS X)



How to Jailbreak with Checkra1n:

1. Download Checkra1n.app on Mac (PC and Linux not yet supported)

2. Connect your device to Mac

3. Click "Start" to allow Checkra1n to put your phone into recovery mode

4. Select "Next" and follow on screen instructions to be put into DFU mode

5. Wait for your device to boot up and you should see the "loader application".

6. Open it and click "Install Cydia"

7. Done! Thats it, repeat the process if device is rebooted.



(Recommended) Please be sure to back up your device data in iTunes before Jailbreaking
